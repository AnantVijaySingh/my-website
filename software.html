I build apps based on two principles:

a. Choose Good: A simple principle—to make the choice that is good. It is easy to spot this by thinking of each choice as one between what is easy and what is right.

b. Less is More: The best of something is in an unadulterated version. Our lives have so much going on. Trying to do 'A' should not have 'B' and 'C' also making a play for our attention and time.
